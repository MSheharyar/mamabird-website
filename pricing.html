Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need a credit card for the free trial?

No. Your 3-month free trial starts the moment you create an account. No payment info needed until you choose to upgrade.

What happens when the trial ends?

You'll see a friendly screen from Chirpy explaining the options. Your child's progress data is saved for 90 days, so nothing is lost if you decide to upgrade later.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes, always. Cancel from your account settings with one click. No questions asked, no penalty, no run-around.

Is this COPPA-compliant?

Yes. Children under 13 do not create their own accounts — all child profiles are created and managed by a parent or teacher. Read our Privacy Policy for full details.

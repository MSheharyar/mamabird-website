The Book

Three Baby
Birdies

Written by Iris Scarfone and illustrated by Ronald Scarfone, Three Baby Birdies is a cheerful, rhythmic tale following three little red birds in their nest on a tree branch — with a bright blue sky, fluffy clouds, and a warm sun shining above.

Written in rhyming language that makes every read-aloud a joy, and illustrated so young children can follow and understand the story on their own terms. Perfect for bedtime, story time, and classroom read-alouds.

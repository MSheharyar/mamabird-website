About the Author

Iris & Frank Scarfone

Iris Scarfone and her husband Frank have always believed the love of reading begins at home. When their son Ronald was a baby, they taught him to read by placing words on posterboards around the house.

That hands-on philosophy became the heartbeat of Three Baby Birdies — short, rhythmic language with illustrations that let young children follow and understand the story on their own terms.

Illustrated by Ronald Scarfone M.S. Reading Education M.L.S. Library Science B.A. Art Education
